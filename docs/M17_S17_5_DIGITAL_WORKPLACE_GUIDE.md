# Sprint 17.5 Digital Workplace Guide

The workplace home combines active targeted announcements, recent activity, published enterprise documents, available workspaces, and communities. Audience rules support organization-wide, named-user, and department targeting. Announcements can be drafts, scheduled, published, or archived and may require acknowledgement.

Communication campaigns record subject, content, channels, target users/departments, schedule, and send state. Sending emits an auditable activity event; production channel delivery must be connected to the notification provider and worker infrastructure.

Employee directory search is tenant restricted. Personal workplace preferences control visible home modules and feed sizing.
